Align Tissue


Alignment takes your experimental tissue sections and helps align them to the Allen Brain Atlas. A predictor network will evaluate your tissue and suggest the best fitting sections. Afterwards you will have the chance to fine tune these predictions to best match your tissue. The results of this step can be used with Count Brain and Cell Detection to quantify your experiment. The intial run of this step requires internet and will take approximately an hour to run its full setup.
